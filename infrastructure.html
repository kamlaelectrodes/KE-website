Infrastructure

Manufacturing and dispatch infrastructure designed to support repeatable welding electrode quality, commercial responsiveness and dependable supply movement for dealers and industrial buyers.

Built for process discipline

Kamla Electrodes operates from its manufacturing base in Achronda Industrial Area, Partapur, Meerut. The facility supports the core stages required for covered welding electrode production: wire preparation, coating, drying, baking, inspection, packaging and dispatch coordination.

The infrastructure is presented here as an operational overview. Detailed plant photography, equipment details, quality-control lab references and capacity information can be shared directly with verified buyers, dealers and institutional procurement teams.
