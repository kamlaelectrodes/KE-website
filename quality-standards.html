Quality & Standards

Quality is not treated as an afterthought. Our process is structured around disciplined inspection, controlled manufacturing, and batch-level confidence for dependable welding performance.

Raw Material Inspection

Incoming steel wire and flux constituents are checked against predefined parameters before release into production.

In-process Controls

Dimensional consistency, coating quality, drying, baking, and moisture control are monitored at critical production stages.

Final Product Testing

Batch verification supports dependable output before dispatch to dealers, fabricators, project customers, and institutional buyers.

Compliance and documentation approach

Kamla Electrodes manufactures covered welding electrodes under applicable Indian quality and product compliance frameworks. Detailed certificates, product test references, and supporting documents are shared directly with verified buyers, authorised procurement teams, and institutional customers when required.

Buyer confidence, without unnecessary public exposure

Certification and license information is included here for traceability and authenticity, while detailed document copies are provided through official communication channels to reduce misuse by unrelated websites or unauthorised sellers.

  • ISO 9001 quality management reference as per company profile records.
  • RDSO Class C2 reference as per company profile records.
  • Product-specific data and technical sheets available on request.
