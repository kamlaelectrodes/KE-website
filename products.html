Safety and handling essentials

  • Use suitable ventilation or local exhaust near the arc.
  • Wear correct eye, face, hand, hearing and body protection.
  • Do not touch live electrical parts.
  • Store packs away from moisture and physical damage.
  • Use qualified procedure guidance for critical or unfamiliar applications.
