Safety and handling essentials

  • Keep your head out of fumes.
  • Use ventilation or exhaust near the arc to keep fumes and gases away from the breathing zone.
  • Wear correct eye, ear and body protection.
  • Do not touch live electrical parts.
  • Protect packages from moisture and physical damage.
