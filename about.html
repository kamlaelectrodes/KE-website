About Kamla Electrodes

A Meerut-based welding electrode manufacturer built on operating continuity, market trust, long-term distribution relationships and practical understanding of industrial welding requirements.

Manufacturing since 1989

Kamla Electrodes (India) was founded in 1989 by Mr. Arun Kumar Mittal. From its Meerut manufacturing base, the company has developed a recognised portfolio serving fabrication, repair, trade, distribution and industrial requirements.

The business combines manufacturing experience with close market relationships. Product development, packing, distribution support and commercial communication are shaped by how electrodes are actually selected, stocked and used across workshops, trade centres, project supply and industrial procurement.
