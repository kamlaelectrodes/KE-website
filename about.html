About the Company

Founded in 1989 by Mr. Arun Kumar Mittal, Kamla Electrodes (India) has grown from a three-member shop to a nationally recognized brand.

Mission: Manufacture and supply reliable, high-quality welding electrodes.

Vision: Expand presence nationally and internationally while fostering a culture of integrity and innovation.

Values: Integrity, Quality, Innovation, Customer Focus.
