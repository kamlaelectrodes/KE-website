About Kamla Electrodes

A Meerut-based welding electrode manufacturer built on long-term market trust, consistent supply relationships and a practical understanding of industrial welding requirements.

Who we are

Kamla Electrodes (India) was founded in 1989 by Mr. Arun Kumar Mittal. Over decades, the company has evolved from a modest manufacturing unit into a recognised welding consumables name serving varied fabrication, trade, repair and industrial requirements.

The company continues to strengthen its product communication, dealer support and buyer response systems while preserving the grounded manufacturing credibility associated with the Kamla Electrodes name.
