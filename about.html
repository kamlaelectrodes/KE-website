About Kamla Electrodes

A Meerut-based welding electrode manufacturer built on long-term market trust, visible operating presence, consistent supply relationships and practical understanding of industrial welding requirements.

Who we are

Kamla Electrodes (India) was founded in 1989 by Mr. Arun Kumar Mittal. Over decades, the company has evolved from a modest manufacturing unit into a recognised welding consumables name serving fabrication, trade, repair, distribution and industrial requirements.

Our identity is grounded in manufacturing continuity, market relationships and a practical understanding of how welding products are selected, stocked and used beyond major metropolitan centres. The company continues to strengthen product communication, distribution support, documentation pathways and buyer response systems while preserving the credibility associated with the Kamla Electrodes name.

Manufacturing Focus

Covered welding electrodes for daily fabrication, structural work, distribution supply and selected application-specific requirements, supported by layered production controls.

Market Support

An India-wide supply outlook with current network strength centred around North and Central India, supported by authorised distribution relationships and direct commercial communication.

Business Accessibility

Quote, distribution, technical, compliance and product-document pathways designed for shopkeepers, fabricators, QR-code users and institutional procurement teams.

Mission

Reliable welding solutions with practical market support

Our mission is to manufacture and support welding consumables with disciplined quality, ethical business practices and responsive communication—helping users choose, source and apply the right product with confidence.

Vision

Build a trusted Indian welding brand with wider reach

Our vision is to expand the Kamla Electrodes presence across India and progressively into wider markets while preserving an inclusive culture, long-term partnerships and the grounded credibility of a manufacturing-led organisation.

Where we are going

Kamla Electrodes is moving toward a more digitally accessible buyer experience: richer product information, better distribution discovery, stronger documentation pathways, QR-enabled support and direct commercial assistance for serious enquiries.
