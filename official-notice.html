Brand & Buyer Protection

Official Notice & Disclaimer

Kamla Electrodes publishes this notice to protect buyers, dealers, procurement teams and market partners from misinformation, unauthorised representation, certification misuse and brand impersonation.

1. Official website and communication

The official website is kamlaelectrodes.com. Buyers, dealers and institutional customers should verify product, pricing, dealership, certification and documentation claims through the official contact details listed on this website.

2. Dealer, seller and city-route verification

Dealer names, locations and availability may change over time. A seller’s listing, market presence or past dealing does not automatically confirm current authorisation. Public cards marked Authorised Distribution Partner represent entries approved for display, but buyers should still confirm current stock and commercial terms.

City enquiry cards are direct enquiry routes to Kamla Electrodes while the public dealer directory is expanded. Their presence does not claim that a dealer has already been appointed in that city.

3. Certification and compliance documents

Compliance references and certification numbers may be shown on this website for traceability. Detailed certificate copies, test references and product-specific documentation are shared through official communication channels for verified commercial, institutional or procurement requirements.

Unauthorised copying, alteration, republication or misuse of Kamla Electrodes certification details, brand assets, product names or company identity is prohibited.

4. Product representation

Only product information confirmed through official Kamla Electrodes communication should be treated as current. Packaging, labels, brands, specifications, availability and portfolio details may be updated from time to time.

5. Website information disclaimer

The website supports business discovery and communication. While care is taken to present reliable information, final commercial, technical and compliance decisions should be based on direct confirmation from Kamla Electrodes.

6. Representative photographs

Where a page uses a clearly described representative stock image, the photograph illustrates an industry environment and does not claim to show a Kamla Electrodes facility, employee, customer or project. Actual company facility photographs are identified separately.

7. Reporting misuse

If you notice a suspicious website, fake dealer claim, misuse of the Kamla Electrodes name, altered certificate, misleading product listing or unauthorised brand representation, contact Kamla Electrodes immediately.
