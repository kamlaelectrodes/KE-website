Official Notice & Buyer Verification

Use this page to confirm official communication routes, authorised-distribution information, quotations, product documentation and legitimate use of the Kamla Electrodes identity.

Effective date: 10 July 2026

1. Official website and email

3. Authorised-distribution verification

A public card marked Authorised Distribution Partner represents a listing approved for display by Kamla Electrodes. Buyers should still confirm current stock, product availability and commercial terms before purchase. A market seller’s past dealing, local presence or third-party listing does not automatically establish current authorisation.

4. National enquiry route

The national enquiry section connects buyers directly with Kamla Electrodes for product, quotation and supply discussions. It is not a representation that a dealer or distributor has been appointed in every location from which an enquiry may be received.

5. Quotations and payment instructions

Treat a price or commercial condition as valid only when issued or confirmed through an official Kamla Electrodes communication channel. Unless clearly stated otherwise in writing, quotations are ex-factory. Freight may be included only when requested and reflected in the written commercial rate.

Before making payment, buyers should verify the quotation, beneficiary details, order reference and payment instructions with the company. Report any unexpected change in bank details or suspicious payment request immediately.

6. Product and certification information

Product classifications, standard references, licence information and portfolio details may be published for buyer convenience. Product-specific TDS, SDS/MSDS, certificates, packing information and supporting documentation are available on request through official channels.

7. Brand, packaging and identity protection

Unauthorised copying, alteration or use of Kamla Electrodes names, logos, product brands, labels, packaging, certificates, website content or company identity is prohibited. No party may claim distributorship, certification, manufacturing association or commercial authority without current written authorisation.

8. Reporting suspected misuse

Please report suspicious websites, altered documents, fake dealership claims, misleading product listings, counterfeit packaging, brand impersonation or unusual payment instructions. Share available screenshots, URLs, seller details, invoices or packet photographs so the matter can be reviewed.

9. Current information

Products, packing, availability, distribution arrangements and commercial conditions may be updated over time. Current written confirmation from Kamla Electrodes takes priority over older website captures, forwarded messages or third-party listings.
