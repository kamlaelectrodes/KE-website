Technical Resources

Practical guidance for electrode handling, storage, product selection, documentation requests and application planning.

Storage & Handling Basics

  • Store electrodes in a dry, moisture-controlled area.
  • Protect packaging integrity to avoid contamination.
  • Follow first-in-first-out stock discipline for dealer and workshop inventory.

Selection Guidance

  • Match electrode size to joint design, base material and job requirement.
  • Use low hydrogen range for critical conditions where applicable.
  • Consult the team for special alloy, hardfacing or repair applications.

Troubleshooting Support

For porosity, arc instability, weld appearance issues or performance concerns, share photos, base material, electrode size and basic weld parameters through the contact form.

What to share for faster technical support

A complete application snapshot helps the team provide practical guidance faster.

  • Product name or electrode type.
  • Size, current setting and welding position.
