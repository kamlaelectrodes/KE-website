Documented Innovation

8 Gauge Short: a smaller dimensional change with a larger market effect

Kamla Electrodes introduced the 4.00 × 350 mm “aath number chota” format after studying wastage and handling in cylinder and related piping fabrication. The solution improved fit with the working cycle and later achieved wider market adoption.
