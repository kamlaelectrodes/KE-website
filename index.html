Product Strength & Quality

Manufacturing capability should be felt in the product.

The clearest proof is what buyers and welders receive in regular use: sound coating condition, dependable arc behaviour, practical slag removal, a presentable bead, protected packing and consistency from batch to batch.

Our quality systems support that product experience. The commercial value is straightforward—a reliable electrode, backed by responsive supply and technical communication, at a deal that makes sense for the work.
