Manufacturing Process

  1. Raw Material Selection: Steel wire and flux chemicals are vetted for quality.
  2. Wire Drawing & Cutting: Drawn to diameter and cut to length per specification.
  3. Flux Coating: Flux prepared; rods coated uniformly.
  4. Baking & Drying: Controlled temperatures remove moisture and ensure shelf-life.
  5. Final Inspection & Packaging: Batch tests precede dispatch.
