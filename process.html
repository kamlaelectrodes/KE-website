Production Overview

Manufacturing Process

A connected route from material receipt and core-wire preparation to coating, moisture control, inspection, packing and dispatch.

1

Material Receipt

Core wire and coating inputs are identified and assigned to the intended production route.

2

Wire Drawing

Wire is reduced and controlled to the required core diameter.

3

Straightening & Cutting

Prepared wire is aligned and cut to the specified electrode length.

4

Coating Preparation

Coating constituents are blended for the applicable product class and extrusion route.

5

Extrusion & Finishing

Coating is applied around the core wire and electrode ends are prepared.

6

Drying & Baking

Moisture is managed and the coating is stabilised for handling and storage.

7

Inspection & Packing

Finished electrodes are reviewed, identified and protected in the appropriate pack.

8

Dispatch Coordination

Order mix, documents, loading and the agreed logistics basis are aligned before movement.
