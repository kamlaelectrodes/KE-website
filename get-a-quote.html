Commercial Terms

How quotations are prepared

Direct bulk quotation support generally starts from a minimum enquiry of 50 boxes. Final MOQ can vary with product mix, size mix, packing and production planning.

Ex-factory by default

Unless specifically written otherwise, quoted prices are ex-factory from the manufacturing location.

Logistics under buyer scope

Freight, transit arrangement, insurance, unloading and destination handling are borne by the buyer under ex-factory supply.

Delivered rate on request

Freight may be included when requested. The selected route and associated logistics cost then become part of the buyer’s quoted rate.

Written confirmation

Availability, taxes, payment terms, dispatch schedule, packing and documentation are confirmed in the final commercial communication.

Additional product, packing, technical and compliance documents are available on request.
