Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for buyers, distribution partners, fabricators and procurement teams evaluating Kamla Electrodes products, applications, documents and supply support.

How do I request a quote?

Use the Get a Quote page and share product or brand, electrode size, quantity, delivery location and company/contact details. Direct bulk quotation support generally starts from 50 boxes. Mention whether the requirement is for workshop supply, project purchase, distribution stock or technical application review so the response can be commercially useful.

How do I find an authorised distribution partner?

Use the Distribution Map to search public authorised entries by city, state, region or product brand. If an authorised entry has not yet been published for your area, use the city enquiry directory to call, email or WhatsApp Kamla Electrodes directly. City enquiry cards are routing tools and do not claim that a dealer has already been appointed.

How can I apply for distributorship?

Share firm details, market area, current welding or industrial product lines, warehouse capability, product interest and estimated monthly capacity. Discussions are reviewed by territory potential, product mix, business fit, long-term seriousness and commercial alignment.

What are E6013 type electrodes generally used for?

E6013 type mild steel electrodes are widely used for general fabrication, light-to-medium structural work, workshop welding, gates, grills, frames, supports, repair jobs and routine metal joining. Final selection should still consider base material, joint design, welding position, current setting, machine characteristics and service requirements.

When should a buyer ask for technical guidance?

Ask for guidance when the job involves uncertain base material, repair-critical conditions, thicker or highly restrained sections, hardfacing, cast iron, low-hydrogen requirements, stainless applications, repeated porosity, cracking, difficult slag removal or uncertain current settings.

What details should I share for product selection?

Share the product name if known, electrode size, base material, approximate thickness, joint type, welding position, machine and polarity, current range, application, quantity, storage history and clear photographs of the job or weld issue where relevant.

What is the difference between a city enquiry card and a dealer listing?

A dealer card marked Authorised Distribution Partner represents a public entry approved for display. A city enquiry card simply connects the buyer to Kamla Electrodes while verified regional dealer data is expanded. It does not make an authorisation claim about that city.
