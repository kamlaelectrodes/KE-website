Frequently Asked Questions

Practical answers for buyers, distribution partners, fabricators and procurement teams evaluating products, capability, documents, commercial terms and supply support.

How do I request a quote?

Use the Get a Quote page and share the brand or product, electrode size, quantity, delivery location, GST/company details and expected buying timeline. Direct bulk quotation support generally starts from 50 boxes, while the final MOQ depends on product mix, size mix, packing and production planning.

Open quote form →

Are quotations ex-factory?

Yes. Unless the quotation specifically states otherwise in writing, prices are ex-factory from the Kamla Electrodes manufacturing location. Freight, transit arrangement, insurance, unloading and destination handling remain under buyer scope.

Can freight be included in the quoted rate?

Yes. A buyer may request a delivered quotation. In that case, the selected logistics route and related freight cost are incorporated into the commercial rate and become part of the buyer’s total cost.

Does Kamla Electrodes maintain capability beyond Six Sigma benchmarks?

Yes. Internal process-performance records support capability above conventional Six Sigma benchmark levels across defined and controlled production parameters. This is presented as part of the company’s quality and prevention-led manufacturing approach.

Detailed calculations, methods and production records are commercially sensitive. A verified customer, auditor, institutional buyer or other legitimate party may request a controlled review through official channels. Disclosure is subject to purpose verification and, where appropriate, an enforceable NDA restricting competitive, adverse and unauthorised use.
