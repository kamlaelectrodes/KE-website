Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for buyers, distribution partners, fabricators and procurement teams evaluating Kamla Electrodes products and supply support.

How do I request a quote?

Use the Get a Quote page and share product type, electrode size, quantity, delivery location and company/contact details. Direct bulk quotation support generally starts from 50 boxes.

Open quote form →

How do I find an authorised distribution partner?

Use the Distribution Map to search by city, state, region or product brand. If your area is not listed yet, send an enquiry and the team will guide you to the nearest suitable supply option.

Open Distribution Map →

How can I apply for distributorship?

Share firm details, market area, product interest and estimated monthly capacity. Distributorship discussions are reviewed by territory potential, product mix, business fit and commercial alignment.

Submit distributorship enquiry →

Which products are currently active?

The active portfolio includes Kmatic Gold, Kmatic X-45, Mahagun, Golden Arc, Lotus, Electra, Koko Tawa Gold, JK, Saurabh 6013 and Electra CocoTawa. Kmatic H 600 is available on request.

Are TDS and SDS/MSDS documents available?

Yes. Product data, TDS, SDS/MSDS, compliance references and technical details can be shared through official Kamla Electrodes communication channels for relevant buyer, distributor and institutional enquiries.
